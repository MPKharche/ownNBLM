# ownNBLM Product Roadmap
## Market-Leading Knowledge Management Platform

---

## Executive Summary

**Vision**: Become the default daily driver for knowledge workers, researchers, and students who need to transform scattered information into actionable intelligence.

**Core USP**: "Your second brain that actually understands context" - Unlike competitors, ownNBLM treats conversations as living knowledge artifacts with deep local-first architecture, full privacy control, and true ownership.

**Target Customers**:
1. **Primary**: Graduate students, PhD researchers, academic professionals
2. **Secondary**: Technical writers, consultants, legal researchers
3. **Tertiary**: Product managers, strategic analysts, lifelong learners

## Phase 1: Foundation Excellence (Months 1-3)
*Make the core experience world-class*

### 1.1 Intelligence Layer
**Problem**: Current AI assistants forget context and don't learn from your knowledge base
**Solution**: Adaptive learning that improves with usage

- [ ] **Smart Document Pre-processing**
  - Auto-extract tables, charts, equations as structured data
  - OCR for scanned documents with quality validation
  - Auto-detect document types and apply specialized parsers
  - Extract and index document metadata (authors, dates, keywords)

- [ ] **Context-Aware Retrieval**
  - Temporal context (recent vs. historical documents)
  - User behavior learning (which sources you trust most)
  - Cross-document relationship mapping
  - Automatic topic clustering across your corpus

- [ ] **Citation Intelligence**
  - Auto-generate bibliography in multiple formats (APA, MLA, Chicago, IEEE)
  - Citation graph visualization (which sources cite which)
  - Dead link detection and archival suggestions
  - Plagiarism detection across your own notes

### 1.2 Annotation System (Current → Enhanced)
**Problem**: Notes scattered across apps don't connect to original sources
**Solution**: Unified annotation layer with AI assistance

- [x] Basic highlights, notes, comments (DONE)
- [ ] **Voice annotations** - Speak notes while reading
- [ ] **Smart tags** - Auto-suggested tags based on content
- [ ] **Linked annotations** - Connect notes across documents
- [ ] **Annotation search** - Full-text search across all your annotations
- [ ] **AI summary** - Generate summaries of your annotation clusters
- [ ] **Spaced repetition** - Auto-schedule review of important highlights

### 1.3 Mind Map Evolution (E-map)
**Problem**: Linear notes don't capture how knowledge connects
**Solution**: Dynamic knowledge graphs that evolve with your understanding

- [x] Basic mind map generation (DONE)
- [ ] **Interactive editing** - Drag, drop, connect, reorganize nodes
- [ ] **AI-suggested connections** - "These concepts might be related"
- [ ] **Multi-session maps** - Merge knowledge from multiple conversations
- [ ] **Concept evolution tracking** - See how your understanding changed over time
- [ ] **Export to popular formats** - Obsidian, Roam, Notion, Miro
- [ ] **Collaborative maps** - Share and co-edit with peers

## Unique Selling Propositions (USPs)

### 1. **True Local-First Architecture**
- **Competitor Weakness**: NotebookLM requires Google account, cloud upload
- **Our Strength**: Full local processing option, zero cloud dependency
- **Market Advantage**: Privacy-conscious users, enterprise customers, regulated industries

### 2. **Conversations → Knowledge Transformation**
- **Competitor Weakness**: Chat history is disposable
- **Our Strength**: Every conversation becomes a reusable knowledge artifact
- **Market Advantage**: Researchers who need to build on previous work

### 3. **PageIndex Vectorless Retrieval**
- **Competitor Weakness**: Vector embeddings require re-indexing, drift over time
- **Our Strength**: Deterministic retrieval, no embedding drift, explainable results
- **Market Advantage**: Legal, academic, compliance use cases requiring auditability

### 4. **Annotation-First Design**
- **Competitor Weakness**: Notes separate from AI interactions
- **Our Strength**: Unified annotation layer across chat and documents
- **Market Advantage**: Active learners who learn by annotating

### 5. **Multi-Modal Mind Mapping**
- **Competitor Weakness**: Linear conversation history
- **Our Strength**: Dynamic knowledge graphs that evolve
- **Market Advantage**: Visual thinkers, complex research projects

### 6. **Open Export Formats**
- **Competitor Weakness**: Proprietary formats, lock-in
- **Our Strength**: Everything exports to markdown, standard formats
- **Market Advantage**: Users who value data ownership

## Risk Mitigation

### Technical Risks
1. **Risk**: PageIndex doesn't scale to millions of documents
   - **Mitigation**: Hybrid approach (PageIndex + vector for huge corpora)

2. **Risk**: Local models too slow on consumer hardware
   - **Mitigation**: Cloud fallback, model quantization, edge deployment

3. **Risk**: Data sync conflicts in team mode
   - **Mitigation**: CRDT-based conflict resolution, Git-like merge UI

### Business Risks
1. **Risk**: OpenRouter/AI vendor pricing changes
   - **Mitigation**: Multi-provider support, local model options, volume discounts

2. **Risk**: Big Tech launches competitive product
   - **Mitigation**: Focus on privacy/local-first moat, specialized features

3. **Risk**: Market too small for venture scale
   - **Mitigation**: Expand TAM with team/enterprise features early
